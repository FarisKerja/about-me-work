About Me
Hi there! My name is Faris and I am a fullstack developer who’s passionate about web and game development. I love diving into new technologies and adapting to different environments. Especially when it helps me grow and chase my dreams.
I’ve been working with tools like Laravel, Vue.js, Tailwind CSS, NestJS, MongoDB, Oracle, Jest, and Ubuntu. I’m really into agile workflows and building efficient backend systems that just work.
I also enjoy learning languages like English. Because I believe the more you learn, the more people and opportunities you can connect with. One of my big goals is to become a game developer, but I still want to stay sharp with web tech so I can balance what I love with what I do.
When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me playing action or racing games or watching movies. Both help me stay creative and motivated.
